About Us

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit that hosts hackathons every 2-3 months on various topics and categories for countless charitable organizations. Our hackathons aim to allow students with a passion for coding and any level of experience to show their skills and contribute to innovation while making an impact.

At Code 4 Hope, we envision a world where technology and creativity unite to drive social change. By empowering students of all skill levels to use their coding talents for the greater good, we aim to create a global community of young innovators solving real-world challenges. Through our hackathons, we provide a platform where passion for technology meets the power of social impact, inspiring the next generation to lead with purpose, compassion, and innovation.

Well, we’re just getting started, but every great journey begins with a single line of code! Code 4 Hope may be new, but we’re fueled by big ideas, passionate coders, and a vision to change the world one hackathon at a time. Stick around – we’re about to make history!
